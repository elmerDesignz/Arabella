About Us

Our JOURNEY

"Arabella" means yielding to prayer. The very essence of Arabella Lifestyle Co came about because the owner did just that, she yielded. "There was a yearning for knowledge about all things natural and an awakening in me that pushed me towards recognizing that God has given us a gift in Mother Earth and every thing we need to ensure we live a wholesome life mentally, physically and emotionally, foods and herbs to heal our bodies, resources to make our surroundings beautiful etc. could be found in Nature. I needed to learn more about this gift, I needed to respect and treasure this gift we have been given I needed to utilize it, I wanted to be around long enough to fulfill my true purpose on this earth.
As my life unfolded, unhealthy circumstances arose. As I yielded to prayer, a truly magnificent journey for me started occurring. Mentorship, Classes, courses, change in diet, mindset change, spiritual development, led to personal development and Healing.... Healing of my Mind, Body & Spirit. I am still on that journey today and while stumbling blocks do occur, I have faith, and my life only promises to become sweeter and sweeter each day for that promise has been given to me by my God in Jeremiah 29:11.
Along this journey, Arabella Naturals Organic Skincare line was birthed, NBN Organic Farms was birthed and the Nurtured by Nature Wellness program was developed, thus, Arabella Lifestyle Co was established through this combination of synergy companies making it the ultimate Wholistic Lifestyle brand. I get you because I have been there.
Rome wasn’t built in a day and as likewise, there are no “quick” fixes to optimal health. Designing a truly naturally healthy lifestyle takes time, effort. energy and commitment, but each small step takes you closer to your goal. We are all for healing and growth. Contact us today, let us help you Design your Naturally healthy Arabella Lifestyle!
